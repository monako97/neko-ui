添加 `block` 属性将使按钮适合其父宽度。

```jsx
<div style={{ display: 'flex', gap: 8, flexDirection: 'column' }}>
  <Button block>一行</Button>
  <Button type="primary" ghost block>
    一行
  </Button>
  <Button type="success" dashed block>
    一行
  </Button>
  <Button type="warning" flat block>
    一行
  </Button>
  <Button link danger block>
    一行
  </Button>
</div>
```
