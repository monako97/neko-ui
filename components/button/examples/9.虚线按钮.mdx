添加 `dashed` 属性将按钮的边框设置成虚线

```tsx
<>
  <Button dashed ghost>
    虚线
  </Button>
  <Button type="primary" dashed ghost>
    虚线
  </Button>
  <Button type="success" dashed ghost>
    虚线
  </Button>
  <Button type="warning" dashed ghost>
    虚线
  </Button>
  <Button type="error" dashed ghost>
    虚线
  </Button>
</>
```
