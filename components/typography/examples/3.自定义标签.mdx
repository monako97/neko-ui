添加 `tag` 属性设置自定义标签类型

```jsx
<>
  <Typography tag="mark">mark</Typography>
  <Typography tag="u">underline</Typography>
  <Typography tag="strong">strong</Typography>
  <Typography tag="del">delete</Typography>
  <Typography tag="i">italic</Typography>
  <Typography tag="kbd">keyboard</Typography>
  <Typography tag="a">link</Typography>
</>
```
