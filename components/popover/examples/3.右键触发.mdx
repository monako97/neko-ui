将 `trigger` 为 `contextMenu`，将可以通过右键触发提示框显示

```tsx
<Popover
  arrow
  content="右键触发"
  trigger="contextMenu"
  className={css`
    width: 100%;
    height: 150px;
    background: var(--disable-bg);
    border-radius: 8px;
    line-height: 150px;
    text-align: center;
    font-size: x-large;
  `}
>
  <div style={{ opacity: 0.4 }}>右键点我</div>
</Popover>
```
